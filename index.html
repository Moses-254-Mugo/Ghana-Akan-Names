Akan Name Calculator

A web application that takes your birthday and calculate the day of the week one was born. It then outputs your Akan name depending on your gender.

Akan people form Ghana frequently name their children after day of the week they were born in. Below is a list of the namings.

Male

  • Sunday - kwasi
  • Monday - Kwado
  • Tuesday - Kwabena
  • Wednesday - Kwaku
  • Thursday - Yaw
  • Friday - Kofi
  • Saturday - Kwama

    • Female

  • Sunday - Akosua
  • Monday - Adwoa
  • Tuesday - Abenaa
  • Wednesday - Akua
  • Thursday - Yaa
  • Friday - Afua
  • Saturday - Ama
